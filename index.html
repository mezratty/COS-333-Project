Elevator Speech

Our campus is an extremely busy place, and it can be hard to keep track of the organized chaos that is student life. With all the fascinating events happening on campus, it is easy to get the room wrong, the time wrong, forget about the event, or not be informed of it in the first place. We wish to create a calendar for student life that solves all of these problems. Implemented in a mobile app, the calendar will include as diverse and inclusive a list of student events as possible, with the intent of catering to all students at Princeton. One especially notable feature is our ticket matchup feature, which connects buyers and sellers of tickets to performances on campus. Right now, students use every listserv imaginable, but PrincetEvents allows users to list themselves as either buying or selling a ticket, and notifies both parties if a match is found!
